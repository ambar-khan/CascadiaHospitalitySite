About Us

Since 2020, Cascadia Hospitality has been yielding inspiration for further travel, providing management services for design and experience driven hotels. Our team has over 45 years of combined hospitality experience with backgrounds in conception, development, operations, marketing and business development.

Meet the Team

Jeremy Duncan

Jeremy brings two decades of experience to Cascadia Hospitality ranging from independent, boutique hotels and restaurants to 300+ room full service properties. He started in operations at Starwood Hotels and quickly transitioned to Sales and Marketing with both Starwood and Marriott International. Back in operations, he is responsible for providing strategic guidance while ensuring superior financial results for independent hotels in Oregon.

Jeremy has earned several certifications from both Marriott International and Starwood Hotels.
